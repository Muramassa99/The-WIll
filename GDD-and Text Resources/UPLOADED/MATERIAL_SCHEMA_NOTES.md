# THE WILL — Material Schema Notes

BaseMaterialDef is universal matter truth.
It applies to weapons, armor, shields, accessories, trinkets, and ranged-support components.

BaseMaterialDef defines:
- physical truth
- direct stat atoms
- capability bias atoms
- skill-family bias atoms
- elemental affinity atoms
- equipment-context bias atoms
- tags / unlock tags / block tags
- economy/lifecycle truth
- visual identity

BaseMaterialDef does NOT define:
- final weapon/armor/accessory classification
- final shape thresholds
- final attack patterns
- final skill lists
- final slot mappings
- center of mass
- reach
- final edge/blunt/pierce profile
- final cooldown feel
- final movement penalty

Those belong to:
- ShapeClassifierResolver
- ProfileResolver
- CapabilityResolver
- BehaviorResolver
