
CORE INTENT

A shared world where:

People matter.

Knowledge matters.

Creation matters.

Seasons create memory.

The game is not about loot.

It is about:

Knowing, doing, and becoming known.

STRUCTURAL SPINE
1. THE CITY (Social Gravity)

A large shared hub.

Not just a lobby — a place where:

People meet.

Guilds form.

Reputations grow.

Crafters become known.

Stories start.

It is the memory bank of the shard.

2. THE FLOORS (Expeditions)

Massive instanced layers.

Each is:

Long-form (15–60 minute journeys)

Multi-layered

Seed-based

Shared effort spaces

They are not “rooms”.

They are places where:

Materials are earned.

Knowledge is tested.

Coordination matters.

Bosses act as social events.

3. CREATION LOOP

Power does not come from drops.

Power comes from:

Turning what you found into something meaningful.

Loop:

Explore / Fight / Gather
→ Bring back parts
→ Craft
→ Trade / Use

Crafting is expressive.

The item matters because of:

What went into it

Who made it

Why it exists

4. IDENTITY

You begin as someone who could be anything.

Your early choices shape:

Efficiency

Leanings

Strengths

Nothing is forbidden.

But intention matters.

Over time:

Players replay not to grind —
but to refine identity.

5. KNOWLEDGE ECONOMY

The true resource is:

Routes

Interactions

Compositions

Coordination

Timing

Social trust

Veterans are powerful because they understand.

Not because they dropped something lucky.

6. SEASONS

Every ~3 months:

Floors reshuffle

Leaderboards reset

First clears matter again

Progress remains.
Memory resets.

Each season becomes:

A new race
with experienced minds.

7. SOCIAL COMPETITION

PvE is the main stage.

But pressure exists.

Optional faction conflict space:

Risk-reward zone

Task-based contribution

Timed multi-faction clashes

Victory comes from:

Organization

Protection

Strategy

Not just damage.

8. RECOGNITION

Titles, first clears, seasonal feats.

Players become known for:

Doing things first

Doing things well

Doing things differently

9. SCALE WITHOUT MMO CHAOS

You are not building an endless overworld.

You are building:

A shared city

Large instanced floors

Scheduled mass events

Community forms within manageable shards.

PLAYER EXPERIENCE

Casual players:

Explore

Gather

Craft

Belong

Focused players:

Specialize

Optimize

Coordinate

Driven players:

Lead

Compete

Become known

OPTIONAL LAYERS (Alive, Not Required)

Secrets and discovery

Movement mastery

Prestige crafting

Social drama

Faction pride

Seasonal rivalries

These create life, not obligation.

PHILOSOPHY

This is not a grind game.

It is:

A cozy, dangerous, collaborative world

Where:

Helping matters

Planning matters

Showing someone a hidden place matters

And where:

Knowledge becomes power
and power becomes reputation.

SINGLE LINE

A seasonal, social dungeon world where players explore vast floors together, transform what they find into meaningful creations, and become known through knowledge, collaboration, and achievement.
